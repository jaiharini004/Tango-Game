🎯 HOW TO PLAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Fill a 6×6 grid using two symbols:
  ☀️ Sun (Yellow)   🌙 Moon (Blue)

Just follow three easy rules:

1️⃣  NO THREE IN A ROW
    You can place two Suns or two Moons next to each other — but never three!
    Example: ☀️☀️🌙 ✅  |  ☀️☀️☀️ ❌

2️⃣  KEEP IT BALANCED
    Every row and every column must have exactly 3 Suns and 3 Moons.
    No more, no less!

3️⃣  FOLLOW THE CLUES
    Some cells are connected by an "×" marker.
    This means those two cells MUST have opposite symbols.
    If one is a Sun, the other must be a Moon!

That's it — no math, no words, just pure logic. 🧠
